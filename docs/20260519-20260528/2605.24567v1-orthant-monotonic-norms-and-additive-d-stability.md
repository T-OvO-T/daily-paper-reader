## 摘要
由向量范数诱导的矩阵测度广泛应用于非线性动力系统的收缩理论中。一个自然且重要的鲁棒性问题是，矩阵测度的负性是否在任意非负对角阻尼下保持不变。具有此性质的矩阵测度被称为可容许的。我们证明，一个诱导矩阵测度是可容许的当且仅当底层范数是象限单调的。等价地，这些正是满足对所有非负对角矩阵D有μ(D) = max_i{d_{ii}}的诱导矩阵测度。我们进一步证明，该类别在非负对角扰动下收缩的一致保持中是最大的。该结果给出了可容许性的新几何刻画，并阐明了象限单调性在加性D稳定性和扩散诱导不稳定性中的作用。

## Abstract
Matrix measures induced by vector norms are widely used in contraction theory of nonlinear dynamical systems. A natural and important robustness question is whether negativity of a matrix measure is preserved under arbitrary nonnegative diagonal damping. Matrix measures with this property have been called admissible. We show that an induced matrix measure is admissible if and only if the underlying norm is orthant-monotonic. Equivalently, these are precisely the induced matrix measures satisfying $μ(D) = \max_i\{d_{ii} \}$ for every nonnegative diagonal matrix $D$. We further show that this class is maximal for uniform preservation of contraction under nonnegative diagonal perturbations. The result gives a new geometric characterization of admissibility and clarifies the role of orthant-monotonicity in additive $D$-stability and diffusion-induced instability.
